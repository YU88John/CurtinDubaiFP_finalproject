What is Millennial?

Millennial Records is a well-known records company established in 1999. The company has four founding members: Minn Thit Kyaw, Hamza Ahmad, Tauqeer Khan, and Rohit Ramnani. In 2010, a group of friends with a similar interest in music had the vision to fulfill their dreams of making it big in the music industry, thus creating Millennial Records. Fast forward 13 years later, Millennial Records is the biggest record company worldwide and is endorsed by celebrities such as Kanye West, Drake, Lil Nas X, and many more.
